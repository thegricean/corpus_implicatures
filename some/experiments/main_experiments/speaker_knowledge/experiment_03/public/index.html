You will read short conversations. These are transcriptions of real conversations that real people had over the phone. The last sentence of each conversation will be marked in red and you will see a blue sentence below the conversation. Your task is to answer whether the blue sentence is true and rate what the person who said the red sentence knows.
The HIT will take approximately 10-15 minutes. Let's start with five examples!

If you have already completed a HIT like this ("Read conversations and rate sentences"), please do not continue.
